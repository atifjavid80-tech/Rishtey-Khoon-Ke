Why Donate Blood?

Your blood donation is a lifeline for someone in need — from accident victims to cancer patients. Every drop counts.

Who Can Donate?

Healthy adults aged 17–65, weighing 50kg+, with no major health restrictions.

What Happens?

Simple check-up, safe collection, and quick recovery with refreshments afterward.

Aftercare

Drink plenty of fluids, avoid heavy exercise, and feel proud — you saved lives!
